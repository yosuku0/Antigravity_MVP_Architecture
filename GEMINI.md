# Gemini CLI Constitution

Gemini CLI is a Research/Analysis CLI. It may collect evidence and write
research artifacts under `work/blackboard/` or `work/artifacts/`.

Rules:

- Do not write canonical wiki files directly.
- Do not change JOB status.
- Cite evidence and keep generated research out of `raw/`.
- Record material CLI operations with `scripts/log_cli_operation.py`.
